Pending changes reviewer

Please read Wikipedia:Reviewing pending changes before requesting this right. Applicants who are new to Wikipedia will not be granted this right. Administrators will only grant this right to applicants who have a measurable track record of editing.

User:MassiveYR

  • MassiveYR (t · c · del · cross-wiki · SUL · xtools · supercount · pages created (xtools • sigma) · non-automated edits · BLP edits · logs (block • rights • moves) · arb · spi) (assign permissions)(notify)
I could use this additional right to help accept or deny pending changes on music-related articles. MassiveYR ? 22:24, 12 May 2017 (UTC)
  Automated comment This user has had 1 request for pending changes reviewer declined in the past 90 days ( [1]). — MusikBot talk 22:30, 12 May 2017 (UTC)
Not done. Your pending changes reviewer rights were removed by administrator Nyttend not more than 2 days prior to this request. They were removed because you created pages that were full of promotional content, such as Seologist and Celoxis (software), as well as copyright violations at Da Capo (musician) and Moneoa Moshesh. Having reviewed the deleted versions of these articles, I agree that they were problematic, and they indicate to me that you may not be familiar enough with Wikipedia's policies and guidelines to appropriately review pending changes. Since these issues were rather recent, restoring the right now would be, in my view, premature. Please take some time continuing to edit constructively and, if you are still interested, re-request the right when you have more experience. Thank you. Mz7 ( talk) 05:24, 18 May 2017 (UTC)
@Mz7: Understood. Thanks. — MassiveYR ? 16:21, 18 May 2017 (UTC)
