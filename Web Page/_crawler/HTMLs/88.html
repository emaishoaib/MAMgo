Template to list useful tools for use in featured article reviews.

Usage

To transclude this template, just place

{{Featured article tools}}

where you want the box on the right to be displayed.

See also

  • Template:Featured article tools/without list (for a smaller selection of FA-related tools)
  • Template:Good article tools
  • Template:Peer review tools
